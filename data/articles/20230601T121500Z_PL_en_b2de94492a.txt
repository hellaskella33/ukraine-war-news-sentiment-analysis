Probably for the first time since the armed assault of Russia on Ukraine, Vladimir Putin's power was questioned on Russian government television - the British Ministry of Defense reports in the daily intelligence update.It is about the speech of the opposition policy of Boris Nadieżdin, who called to choose the new President of Russia in 2024.

Nadieżdin said that with "current power in Moscow, Russia cannot count on returning to Europe."The oppositionist talked about the war in Ukraine and called for the election of a new president in 2024 in order - as he said - rebuilding normal relations with Europe.
